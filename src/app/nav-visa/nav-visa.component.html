Visitor Visa

The Visit Visa also known as Tourist Visa is a type of non-immigrant visa for individuals desiring to enter a country of their choice, temporarily for different purposes such as business, pleasure, tourism or medical treatment, visiting friends or family and family re-union for a restricted period of time.
